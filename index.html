The Music of Andy Williams

Image source: Wikimedia Commons

Andy Williams, born Howard Andrew Williams, was a popular singer between the 1960s and 1970s. During his career, he created 43 studio albums, eight of which were Christmas albums. Williams primarily created music that was in the Easy Listening genre. This quality makes listening to Williams' music relaxing and good background noise for everyday tasks.
